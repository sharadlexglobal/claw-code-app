# Content Repurpose Engine (Content Accordion)

One raw legal input becomes 10+ polished content pieces across all platforms. Based on Alex Hormozi's Content Accordion method adapted for Indian legal education.

## The Content Accordion Flow

### Input: ONE Legal Topic
Example: "Section 498A IPC — Cruelty by husband and his family"

### Expansion into 10 Formats

**1. Instagram Post** (150-250 words)
- Hook: Myth-bust or surprising fact about the topic
- Body: Feynman-style simple explanation
- Takeaway + educational CTA

**2. Instagram Carousel** (8-10 slides)
- Slide-by-slide breakdown of the concept
- Each slide = one micro-point
- Visual-friendly text density

**3. Instagram Reel Script** (90 sec, TTS-clean)
- Spoken conversational explanation
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Zero visual directions

**4. LinkedIn Post** (800-1200 chars)
- Professional analysis angle
- Thought leadership positioning
- Ends with discussion question

**5. X/Twitter Thread** (5-8 tweets)
- Bite-sized points, 280 chars each
- Thread narrative arc
- Standalone value per tweet

**6. Blog Article** (1500-2200 words)
- SEO-optimized deep dive
- H2/H3 structure
- FAQ section

**7. Quora Answer** (400-700 words)
- Direct answer first
- Structured explanation
- Practical takeaway

**8. Reddit Post** (300-600 words)
- ELI5 opening
- Community-friendly tone
- TL;DR at end

**9. Google Business Update** (100-250 words)
- Local Delhi NCR angle
- Tied to specific legal service area

**10. Podcast Notes**
- Episode title + summary
- Timestamped topics
- Quotable moments

## Angle Variation per Platform

The SAME topic gets different angles per platform:

| Platform | Angle | Tone |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram Post | Myth-bust, quick insight | Friendly, Hindi-English |
| Carousel | Step-by-step breakdown | Educational, visual |
| Reel | Conversational explainer | Speaking directly to camera |
| LinkedIn | Professional analysis | Formal, English-dominant |
| X/Twitter | Quick facts, thread | Punchy, concise |
| Blog | Comprehensive guide | SEO, authoritative |
| Quora | Answer a specific question | Helpful, detailed |
| Reddit | Community education | Casual, genuine |
| Google Business | Local relevance | Professional, local |
| Podcast | Deep discussion prep | Conversational, detailed |

## Quality Rules
1. Each format must feel NATIVE to its platform — not a copy-paste
2. Same core facts and citations, different presentation style
3. Hooks must be unique per platform — never repeat the same hook
4. Platform-specific word limits must be respected
5. All formats independently valuable — someone should be able to consume just one
6. Consistency in facts — never contradict across formats

## Content Batch Efficiency
When generating all 10 formats:
1. Start with the blog (deepest research)
2. Extract key points for carousel and thread
3. Simplify for Instagram post
4. Convert to spoken script for reel
5. Formalize for LinkedIn
6. Answer-ify for Quora
7. Community-ify for Reddit
8. Localize for Google Business
9. Structure for podcast notes
