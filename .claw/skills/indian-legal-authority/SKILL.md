# Indian Legal Authority & Compliance Engine

Ensures every piece of legal content is backed by specific law citations and complies with Bar Council of India advertising rules.

## BCI Rule 36 Compliance (MANDATORY)

### NEVER include
- "Contact us for consultation"
- "DM for legal help"
- "Book an appointment"
- "Call us at [number]"
- Any CTA that seeks clients or promotes legal services
- Celebrity endorsements or promotional content
- Pricing of legal services

### ALWAYS use instead
- "Save this for when you need it"
- "Share with someone who should know this"
- "Follow for daily legal knowledge"
- "Comment mein batao — kya ye aapko pata tha?"
- "Knowledge of your rights is your first line of defense"

## Citation Standards

### Section References
Format: "Section [number] of [Full Act Name], [Year]"
- "Section 498A of the Indian Penal Code, 1860"
- "Section 125 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973"
- "Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881"

For spoken/TTS content, spell out: "Section four-ninety-eight-A of the Indian Penal Code"

### Judgment Citations
Format: "[Party 1] vs [Party 2] ([Year]) [Court]"
- "Arnesh Kumar vs State of Bihar (2014) Supreme Court"
- "Navtej Singh Johar vs Union of India (2018) Supreme Court"
